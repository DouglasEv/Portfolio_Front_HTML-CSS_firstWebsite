Batman and Joker in Leage of Legend

Vayne: One Of The Most Popular Marksmen

Vayne is extremely interactive with a great deal of outplay potential. While she is a 'late game' champion, her scaling is very strong and is one of the, if not the best carry champion in the game.

Shaco: The Demon Jester

Shaco is an insane individual, committing atrocities just because he feels like it. He delights in others' pain and often finds what he does funny. There is no real motive to his crimes.
